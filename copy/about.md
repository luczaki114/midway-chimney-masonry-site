### The Story

**Headline:** Where We Come From

**Body:**

We started this business near Midway Airport because this is where we're from. The South Side of Chicago — the bungalows, the two-flats, the older brick buildings that have been holding up Chicago families for a hundred years. We know this housing stock. We know what Chicago winters do to mortar joints, to chimney crowns, to the steel lintels above a vintage window. We've been fixing it for over 25 years.

Midway Chimney & Masonry grew out of a simple idea: that homeowners deserve a contractor who tells them the truth, shows up when they say they will, and charges a fair price. It sounds straightforward. In this industry, it turns out to be rare.

We've cleaned chimneys in Beverly and Bridgeport. We've repointed brickwork in Oak Lawn and Orland Park. We've replaced I-beams and shelf angles on three-flats in the near South Side. We've done it all under one name, with the same family doing the work — no subcontractors, no call centers.

### What We Believe

**Headline:** How We Think Work Should Be Done

**Item 1 — Tell people what things cost.**

Before we ever show up at your home, you know what you're paying. We publish our prices because we believe pricing transparency is the bare minimum of honest business. No surprise charges when we're done. No pressure to add on work you didn't ask for.

**Item 2 — Show up when you say you will.**

We confirm appointments. We keep them. If something changes, we call you — not the other way around. That's not exceptional service. That's just showing up.

**Item 3 — Tell the truth about what you find.**

After a 14-point chimney inspection, we tell you exactly what we found — good and bad. If your chimney is fine, we'll say so. If there's a problem, we'll describe it clearly and tell you what it would cost to fix. Then the decision is yours. No pressure, no invented urgency.

**Item 4 — Do both sides of the work.**

We built Midway to handle everything your home's brick and chimney system needs — from a fireplace sweep to a parapet wall rebuild. That's not common. Most chimney sweeps don't do structural masonry. Most masonry contractors don't touch chimneys. We do both, because splitting the work between two contractors costs you more and creates gaps in accountability.
