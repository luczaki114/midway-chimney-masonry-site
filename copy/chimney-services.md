### Service: Chimney Liner System Installation

**Body:**

Your chimney liner is the inner sleeve that contains combustion gases and directs them safely out of your home. Old clay tile liners crack. Older homes often have no liner at all. A failed or missing liner is a safety hazard — full stop. We install stainless steel flex liners for wood-burning fireplaces, gas appliances, and oil furnaces. Installation is sized to your specific appliance and flue, not a one-size-fits-all fix.

**Body:**

### Service: Chimney Rebuilds

**Body:**

When a chimney crown is gone, mortar joints are shot, and bricks are crumbling, a patch won't hold. A rebuild does. We tear down deteriorated chimney sections to sound brick and rebuild from there — new mortar, new bricks where needed, new crown, properly flashed and capped. We rebuild to current code standards so you're not just restoring what was there, you're improving it. This is the last repair a chimney should need for decades.
