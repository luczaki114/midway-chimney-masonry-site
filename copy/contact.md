**Copywriter's Note:** The service dropdown on the contact form includes "Other / Not Sure" deliberately — a significant share of homeowners know something is wrong but don't know what it's called. Removing that barrier reduces abandonment. The dropdown also replaces the original site's open "Subject" text field, which is ambiguous friction; a dropdown lets Midway pre-route inquiries and signals service breadth at a glance. The senior/veteran note at the bottom of the page is a standalone element so it doesn't get lost in the form section — it's a rare differentiator and should be visible to anyone who scrolls the page.
