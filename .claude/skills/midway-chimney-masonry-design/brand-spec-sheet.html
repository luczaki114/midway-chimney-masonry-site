COMPONENTS05
Applied · Photo treatment

Real job sites, one warm overlay.

  1. Warm near-black overlay at 40–55% on every photo background.
  2. Photos only on dark sections — never on a light background.
  3. Bleed to the edge: no rounded corners, no border. Constrain the text, not the image.

Authentic, warm, daylight photography only. No stock — it clashes with the real work.

PHOTO TREATMENT06
Reference

Hard rules

Always
  • Warm palette only — serif headlines, Inter body (16px+).
  • Alternate dark / light sections down the page.
  • Photos on dark sections, under a 40–55% warm overlay, edge-to-edge.
  • Fire red on CTAs & prices; oxblood for destructive.
  • Flat surfaces, 6px radius; one shadow (sticky nav).
  • Deliberate 250–300ms ease-in-out; buttons deepen on hover.
  • Transparent pricing in the serif, at menu-price scale.
Never
  • Navy blue — anywhere.
  • Badge / certification logo grids.
  • Pill-shaped buttons.
  • Thin-weight Playfair.
  • Stock photography.
  • "Family Owned" as a badge or stamp.
  • Gradients, patterns, textures, glassmorphism.
  • Emoji · icon grids · auto-playing video.

Voice: neighborly, honest, plain-spoken South Side Chicago. "We" for the business, "you" for the reader. Sentence case; UPPERCASE only for the tracked eyebrow. Specificity over claims — name the neighborhood and the trade detail. No hype, no corporate filler.
